Privacy Protocol

Effective: Dec 17, 2025 Dev: NovaSphere

1. The Core Philosophy: "Zero-Knowledge"

NovaWallet is architected on a "Zero-Knowledge" and "Offline-First" philosophy. Unlike standard financial applications, NovaWallet does not rely on a central cloud server to store your financial ledger.

We cannot sell your data because we do not possess it.

Your financial transactions, client lists, invoices, and debt tracking records exist physically on your device’s local storage. NovaSphere Studio has no technical means to access, view, or decrypt your personal financial database.

2. Data Storage & Encryption

2.1 Local Storage

All application data is stored locally on your device in an SQLite database.

  • Encryption: This database is encrypted at rest using SQLCipher (AES-256 bit encryption).
  • Key Management: The decryption key is generated via hardware entropy and stored securely in the Android Keystore or iOS Keychain. This key is only released when you successfully authenticate via Biometrics (Fingerprint/FaceID) or Device PIN.

2.2 The "Sovereign Cloud" (Backup & Sync)

NovaWallet offers an optional cloud synchronization feature. This feature is user-initiated.

  • Transport Security: Before your data leaves your device, it is serialized into a JSON blob and encrypted with a secondary, user-defined password using AES-GCM.
  • Destination: You may choose to upload this encrypted file to your personal Google Drive or save it locally for manual export.
  • No Intermediary: The encrypted file moves directly from your device to your Google Drive. It never passes through NovaSphere Studio servers.

3. Data Collection & Usage

We collect minimal data strictly for the functionality of the application.

Data Type Storage Location Purpose
Financial Ledger Local Device Only To track income, expenses, and net worth.
Receipt Images Local Device (Sandboxed) To attach proof of purchase to transactions.
Client Details Local Device Only To generate invoices and track trust scores.
Crash Logs Anonymized / Optional To identify application stability issues.

4. Third-Party Services & APIs

4.1 Google Drive API

Scope: We request the drive.file scope. This limits NovaWallet’s access ONLY to files created by NovaWallet. We cannot see, read, or modify your other Google Drive files, photos, or emails.

Usage: Used strictly for the "Sovereign Cloud" backup feature.

4.2 Payment Gateways (Paystack)

Usage: To process payments for "Nova Ultra" (Pro) subscriptions.

Data: Payment processing is handled entirely by Paystack. NovaWallet does not store your credit card details. We only receive a cryptographic token confirming that a payment was successful.

6. System Interactions

NovaWallet interacts with other apps on your device to facilitate your workflow.

  • Phone & Email: The Client Management module allows you to tap a client's number to open your Dialer or their email to open your Mail App. We do not record your calls or read your emails.
  • Web Browser: Used to open the Privacy Policy and to complete payment transactions via Paystack securely.

7. Regulatory Compliance (GDPR & CCPA)

Since NovaWallet is offline-first, you have absolute control over your data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your data within the app at any time.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your entire ledger to CSV or JSON via the "Data Grid" export feature.
  • Right to Erasure: To permanently delete your data, simply uninstall the application. Since we hold no copies on our servers, uninstalling the app destroys the encryption keys and renders the local database unrecoverable.

8. Children’s Privacy

NovaWallet is a financial tool intended for use by adults or minors under guardian supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
