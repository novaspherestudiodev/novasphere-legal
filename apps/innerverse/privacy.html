Privacy Protocol

Updated: Dec 19, 2025 InnerVerse OS

1. The "Offline-First" Promise

Our core philosophy is Privacy by Design. InnerVerse is architected as an offline-first sanctuary. We do not create user accounts, we do not run tracking servers, and we have no access to the thoughts, memories, or reflections you create within the app.

1.1 User Content

All content you create—including Bloom Realm ideas, Echo Realm messages, and Spectral AR location tags—is stored locally on your device.

  • We do not transmit this data to any cloud server.
  • We do not have access to read, view, or analyze your content.

1.3 Bio-Rhythmic Data

The "Bio-Sync" feature allows you to tap a rhythm on the screen to synchronize the app’s visuals. This data is manual input only. We do not access your device's physical biometric sensors (fingerprint scanner, optical heart rate sensor) nor do we collect health data.

2. Permissions & Sensors

We request specific permissions to enable our "Alien Tech" features. These are used only when you initiate an action.

Location (Spectral AR) Used exclusively for the Echo Realm. Allows you to "anchor" a memory to a specific GPS coordinate. Your location is processed locally to calculate distance. We do not track movements.
Sensors (Magnetometer) Used to determine compass direction for the Spectral AR Scanner and the Living Biome parallax effects.
Camera & Microphone Used to record "Temporal Duets" and display the AR View. We do not stream or upload these feeds.
Internet Access Used only for processing "Supporter" payments and sending anonymous usage stats (e.g., "App Opened"). We never sync your journal entries.
File Storage Used to Export your data (JSON backup) or save generated media files to your device.
Exact Alarms Used to wake your device at the exact moment a scheduled "Echo" is set to unlock, ensuring your messages from the past arrive with precise timing.
2.2 On-Device Intelligence (The "Prophecy Engine")

InnerVerse features "Oracle" and "Prophecy" features that provide insights. No Cloud Processing: All logic, pattern recognition, and text generation happen locally on your device using pre-defined algorithms. No Training: Your data is never used to train external AI models.

3. Payments & Services

InnerVerse offers an optional "Supporter" program.

  • Processing: We use Paystack (or Google Play Billing) to process payments securely.
  • Privacy: NovaSphere Studio does not store your credit card details. We receive only a confirmation receipt to unlock features.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Manual Deletion: You can delete specific items at any time.
  • Automated Entropy: If you enable "Ephemeral Mode" on an Echo, the app automatically deletes that entry after a set number of views.
  • Reset App: The Settings menu includes a "Reset App" feature which permanently wipes all local database content.

5. Children’s Privacy

InnerVerse is suitable for users of all ages (Rated 12+ for themes). We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. Since the app works offline and requires no account, children can use the app safely without risking data exposure.
