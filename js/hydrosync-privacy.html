HydroSync Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

Medical Disclaimer
HydroSync is a wellness tool, not a medical device. The hydration recommendations provided are estimates based on general algorithms. Always consult a physician for medical advice.

1. Health Data Collection

Unlike other apps, HydroSync processes your health data (weight, step count) strictly on your device.

  • We do not upload your health metrics to our servers.
  • We utilize Apple HealthKit and Google Fit APIs solely to read your step count locally.

2. Location Services

To provide accurate weather-based hydration goals, we access your approximate location. This data is sent to the OpenWeatherMap API solely to retrieve temperature data and is not stored.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

4. Contact Us

For privacy concerns regarding HydroSync, contact: privacy@novasphere.studio
